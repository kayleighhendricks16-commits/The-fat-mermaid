WELCOME TO PARADISE

About Little Mykonos

Nestled within The Fat Mermaid estate, Little Mykonos offers a serene escape inspired by the Greek islands. Our Cycladic-style suites blend modern comfort with timeless elegance, providing the perfect backdrop for a relaxing getaway.

Whether you're here for a romantic weekend, a family vacation, or a wellness retreat, you'll find everything you need to unwind. From our sparkling pool to our on-site day spa, every detail is designed to transport you to the Mediterranean.

ACCOMMODATION

Comfort & Style

Welcome to our comfortable and stylish accommodation, where convenience, relaxation, and thoughtful details come together to create a memorable stay. Our rooms are designed to provide guests with a cozy yet modern space that is both functional and beautifully presented.
